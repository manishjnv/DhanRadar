# Compliance Policy (F1, F12) — research, not advice

## Posture
DhanRadar is a **research analytics platform**, NOT an investment advisor and NOT a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser (RIA). We provide **data, scores, and education** — never personalized buy/sell advice.

## Hard rules (product + copy)
- Signals (Strong Buy/Buy/Hold/Caution/Avoid) describe the **Score band**, not a recommendation to act. Copy uses "scores", "ranks", "signals" — never "we recommend you buy".
- Every AI output and recommendation surface carries: **"Not investment advice. Markets carry risk."**
- No personalized portfolio advice. Portfolio Insights are **observations**, never instructions ("Top 3 = 58% concentration", never "sell X").
- If we ever offer advice features → register as SEBI RIA first (separate legal track).

## SEBI Research Analyst (RA)
- If we publish research reports/ratings, evaluate **SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations 2014** applicability. Partner with a registered RA or register before publishing rated research. **Gate: legal sign-off before launch.**

## Disclaimers (placement)
- Footer (all pages), every AI answer, every recommendation card, onboarding consent, email footers.
- Methodology page (public) explains Score construction transparently.

## Payments / GST (F12)
- Amounts in integer paise; **GST 18%** computed server-side, shown inclusive with a breakdown on invoice.
- Invoices immutable; refunds create a negative invoice + audit entry. Razorpay as gateway (UPI/cards/netbanking/autopay).

## Launch gate (P0)
- [ ] Legal review of all signal/advice copy
- [ ] RA applicability determination + sign-off
- [ ] Disclaimer audit across surfaces
