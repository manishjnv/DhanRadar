# Executive Analytics Dashboard

**Audience:** Founders/CTO/Exec. **Cadence:** daily glance + weekly review. (Complements /observability dashboard-specs Executive view — this is the growth lens.)

## Tiles
1. **North Star:** WRI (current, WoW, trend).
2. **Revenue:** MRR, ARR, MoM growth, ARPU.
3. **Funnel:** visit→signup→activation→paid (rates + WoW).
4. **Acquisition:** signups/wk, channel mix, CAC, K-factor.
5. **Activation:** rate, TTFV.
6. **Retention:** D30 by cohort, DAU/MAU.
7. **Subscription:** Free→Pro, trial→paid, churn, net revenue retention.
8. **Engagement:** rec CTR/conversion, AI adoption + helpful-rate, portfolio-synced %.
9. **Unit economics:** LTV:CAC, AI ₹/user, gross margin.
10. **Health:** availability, major incidents (link to /observability).

## Segments/filters
- Time range, acquisition channel, persona, plan, platform.

## Cadence rituals
- Weekly growth review: North Star + funnel drop-offs + active experiments. Monthly: cohort retention + LTV:CAC + roadmap impact.
